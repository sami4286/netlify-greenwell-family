Finances & Insurance

No insurance no problem

Financial matters shouldn't be an obstacle to getting the dental care you and your family need considering how important your oral health is to your overall health. As a new patient, we understand that it can be difficult to afford the cost of dental care. That is why we at Greenwell Springs Family Dentistry are here to work with you to ensure that you can maintain the highest level of oral health without worrying about the price of dental services.

At Greenwell Springs Family Dentistry, we offer a variety of payment options, including various payment plans, and also accept Visa, MasterCard, and CareCredit, so that you can find the most suitable solution for your budget. Our services are tailored to fit your needs, so we will work with you to ensure that you get the care you need at an affordable price. We are committed to helping you keep your mouth in top condition with our affordable dentist.

Discount Dental Plan

At Greenwell Springs Family Dentistry, we offer high-quality dental care services at prices that work for any budget. It is our goal to make sure that your family has access to the dental care that you need and deserve. With our Discount Dental Plan, you can easily afford the dental care that you and your family need.

We understand that dental care can be expensive, which is why we offer CareCredit financing. CareCredit lets you pay for your dental care over time and helps you spread out the cost of your dental care. With CareCredit, you can make payments that fit into your budget and get the dental care that you need.

At Greenwell Springs Family Dentistry, we are committed to providing our patients with the dental care that they need at prices that are affordable. Our Discount Dental Plan provides you with quality dental care at prices that are low. For more information on our Discount Dental Plan and CareCredit financing, please contact one of our team members or visit our website for more details.
